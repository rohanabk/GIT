Selection process for Hackathon

(Pre-Hackathon)

Pre-Hackathon process involves 2 phases:

Phase 1 : Ideas/proposals presentation competition

Students who are already having ideas/proposals will present the same Ideas/proposals presentation competition. The presentations will be evaluated with respect to various parameters like Originality of the idea, Relevance of problem in regional /societal context, Feasibility of solution, Inclusion of IT component, and academic results. Short listed students may be interviewed to identify Entrepreneurial skills if necessary. Students more than cut off points will be selected for Hackathon.

Ideas addressing regional problems/societal needs will have high priority.

Phase 2 : Qualifying test

Small Presentation by the students to the monitoring committee has to be done by few shortlisted students for the purpose of more clarity.

Rs. 3 Lakhs funding

College level monitoring committee and state level steering committee selected 10 projects after Hackathon from GIT, Incubation Center. Upto 3 Lakhs funding was done by KBITS, Govt of Karnataka for projects/proposals selected for Incubation center upon approval of screening committee. Each team was assigned a Mentors and Guide during the execution of project and they guide the teams in carrying out their project as per the guidelines. The regional coordinator, college coordinator and monitoring committee reviewed and monitor the Progress of the project during execution stage and submit the reports to Steering Committee.
